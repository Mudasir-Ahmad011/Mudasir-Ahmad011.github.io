Console-Based HMS

Application 4

The Hospital Management System is a computer program designed to streamline hospital operations as manual management becomes challenging. It's a time-saver for both patients and staff, eliminating the need for patients to wait in long queues. This system primarily deals with patient management, allowing the staff to handle various tasks like generating reports, suggesting medications, and calculating bills. The staff uses this system to perform basic patient data operations known as CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete). Additionally, they oversee the scheduling of doctors and appointments. Patients can access their prescriptions, medical reports, and bills through the system. Moreover, for common diseases, the system offers basic guidance. Its automation of patient information and medical records helps the hospital operate more efficiently, reducing errors and enhancing financial management.

Console-Based Speed Demons

Game 2

Welcome to 'Speed Demons,' a high-speed car racing game set in a futuristic virtual world. Your mission as the player, known as Ghost Rider, is to conquer the racing circuit by eliminating demon racers. In the year 2060, the introduction of high-tech cars revolutionized the world of racing, leading to the ultimate race series, 'Speed Demons,' where top demon racers compete for glory. You, as Ghost Rider, an unknown rookie, aim to climb the ranks by defeating enemies. Armed with one life, your task is to shoot down enemy cars to score 100 points for each successful hit. The game comprises two levels of escalating difficulty. The initial stage features enemies like Kratos, Zeus, and Hades, who shoot lasers. Colliding with enemy cars results in a 20% health deduction, while their bullets decrease your health by 10% per hit. The final stage presents a formidable foe—the Police Car. This adversary can move in both horizontal and vertical directions. If its bullet hits your car, you lose 30% health. To progress, you must clear each level while avoiding collisions with other cars or bullets. The ultimate goal is to eliminate all demon racers and defeat the Police Car to emerge victorious in the game.

Documentary

Welcome to "Breaking the Chains," a powerful documentary that delves deep into the harrowing world of drug addiction, exploring it from the unique perspectives of an individual struggling with this disease, the Islamic viewpoint, and insights from expert psychologists. Join us as we unravel the complexities of addiction and provide a comprehensive understanding of this global issue.
🎥 Part 1: "My Battle Within" 🎥
In the first segment, we hear the heartfelt and candid account of a courageous individual who has experienced the gripping reality of drug addiction firsthand. Witness the emotional journey of their fight against addiction, as they share personal insights, challenges, and breakthroughs. Their story will touch your heart and illuminate the importance of empathy and support in the path to recovery.
🕌 Part 2: "Islam's Perspective on Addiction" 🕌
Drawing from the rich spiritual wisdom of Islam, we explore the Islamic perspective on addiction and its impact on individuals and communities. Expert Islamic scholars shed light on the moral and ethical aspects of substance abuse, addressing its consequences and the importance of compassion and guidance in dealing with those affected.
💡 Part 3: "The Psychology Behind Addiction" 💡
In this thought-provoking segment, distinguished psychologists shed light on the psychological aspects of addiction. Discover the underlying factors that contribute to substance dependence, the brain's role in addiction, and the behavioral patterns that perpetuate the cycle. We explore the different treatment approaches, highlighting the significance of therapy, counseling, and support networks.
🌟 Part 4: "Seeking Recovery and Hope" 🌟
In the final chapter, we emphasize the significance of seeking help and the process of recovery. We showcase success stories of individuals who have triumphed over addiction with the combined support of faith, psychology, and self-determination. This section aims to inspire and provide a glimmer of hope for those struggling with addiction, showing that healing and transformation are indeed possible.
"Breaking the Chains" is a documentary that seeks to foster understanding, compassion, and dialogue about drug addiction. We aim to raise awareness about this pervasive issue, empower those affected, and encourage communities to offer support and assistance to those on the path of recovery. Together, we can break the chains of addiction and create a more compassionate, informed, and empathetic society.
